
How I lightened the background of some images in Gimp.
  Layer. Duplicate Layer
  Layer. Mask. Apply Layer Mask
    Choose [Grayscale copy of layer] and check the [Invert Mask] option.
  In the layers dialogue change the Mode of the duplicated layer to Screen.
  File. Overwrite [filename]


How to create black and white version.
  Colours. Desaturate.
    Choose between Lightness, Luminosity, Average
  Colours. Brightness Contrast. Increase brightness to max.
  File. Overwrite [filename]
